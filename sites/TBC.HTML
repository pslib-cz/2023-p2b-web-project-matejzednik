The Burning Crusade

Theme of the expansion

The shattered world of Outland, once the sacred realm of the Draenei, now lies in ruins due to the demonic influence of the Burning Legion.

New features

New races emerge—the Blood Elves and Draenei—each with their unique abilities and rich lore. Soar above the broken landscape on majestic flying mounts. Brave the challenges of Heroic dungeons and epic raids.
