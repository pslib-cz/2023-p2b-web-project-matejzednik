The Wrath of the Lich King

Theme of the expansion

The Shadowlands expansion delves into the realm of death, disrupting the cosmic balance.

New features

Unleash the power of fallen knights as a Death Knight — a new hero class. Ascend the frozen spires of Icecrown Citadel in pursuit of the Lich King.

Covenants: Align with one of four Covenants, each granting unique abilities and storylines.

Torghast, Tower of the Damned: A procedurally generated mega-dungeon.

Level Squish: The first-ever level squish reduces the level cap from 120 to 60.

6 New Zones: Explore the realms of the Shadowlands, including Bastion, Maldraxxus, Ardenweald, and Revendreth.
